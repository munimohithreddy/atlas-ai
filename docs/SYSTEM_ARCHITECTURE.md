# Atlas AI System Architecture

Atlas follows an Opportunity -> BusinessPlan -> Campaign -> CampaignTask -> CampaignAsset -> Distribution -> Analytics -> Learning flow.

Campaigns progress through Approved -> Task Execution -> Dependency Readiness -> Progress -> Campaign Ready before future asset production and publication milestones.

## Brand

The ownership layer. A brand groups related opportunities and future campaigns around a market and audience.

## Opportunity

The decision layer. Opportunities capture evaluated topics, scores, evidence, and ranking outputs.

## BusinessPlan

The planning layer. A business plan chooses monetization, acquisition channels, expected revenue, effort, assets, and next action.

## Campaign

The execution layer. Campaigns turn approved business plans into tracked work with human approval before execution.

## CampaignTask

The work-planning and execution layer. Tasks define the ordered execution steps that prepare a campaign and track progress through deterministic states.

## CampaignAsset

The asset-planning layer. Assets define what should be produced for each campaign channel.

## Assets

The creation layer. Assets include websites, guides, comparison pages, social assets, and lead capture resources.

## Distribution

The publishing layer. External platforms are treated as acquisition channels and distribution surfaces.

## Analytics

The measurement layer. Analytics tracks traffic, clicks, revenue, conversion, and return on effort.

## Learning

The improvement layer. Atlas will use outcomes from execution to improve future recommendations.
