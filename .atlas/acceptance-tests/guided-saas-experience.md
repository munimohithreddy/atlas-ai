# Guided SaaS Product Experience Acceptance Test

Run this on a clean local database.

1. Open the empty dashboard.
2. Confirm onboarding is shown instead of zero-count metrics.
3. Create an opportunity.
4. Confirm the opportunity card explains score, demand, competition, monetization, linked plan, linked campaigns, and next action.
5. Create a business plan from the opportunity.
6. Confirm the business plan shows the linked opportunity.
7. Create a campaign from the business plan.
8. Confirm the campaign shows linked opportunity and business plan context.
9. Approve the campaign.
10. Confirm the first task becomes Ready to Start.
11. Confirm the first task does not show Dependency: None.
12. Start the first task.
13. Block and unblock the task.
14. Complete the task using decimal actual hours such as 1.5.
15. Confirm the next dependent task unlocks.
16. Confirm a blocked task does not unlock its dependent task.
17. Confirm a cancelled dependency does not unlock its dependent task.
18. Open the campaign Assets tab.
19. Confirm every asset has a purpose, type, channel, status, priority, and valid action.
20. Add an asset to the production queue.
21. Start production.
22. Send it for review.
23. Request changes.
24. Resume production.
25. Send it for review again.
26. Approve it.
27. Confirm Ready to Publish.
28. Confirm Production Queue placement at every stage.
29. Confirm queue filters use dropdown controls.
30. Confirm Clear Filters resets queue filters.
31. Confirm queue item action opens the related campaign Assets tab.
32. Confirm dashboard metrics and next recommended action update from real state.
33. Confirm no raw underscore enum values appear in normal UI.
34. Confirm Documentation explains product terms in plain language.
35. Confirm the layout is readable at desktop, tablet, and mobile widths.
